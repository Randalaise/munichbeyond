What is MUNICH Beyond?

MUNICH Beyond brings together festivals, venues and cultural institutions from Munich and Bavaria with artists, creators and producers from around the world to explore the future of immersive arts.

Expect XR works, showcases, talks and encounters — where artistic practice meets real-time media and new audiences.

What to Expect

MUNICH Beyond offers a curated program at the intersection of immersive arts and production cultures.

A Short Look Back

MUNICH Beyond took place for the first time in 2025, bringing together immersive works, professional exchange and an international community.

The 2026 edition builds on this first edition with an expanded program and new international partnerships.
